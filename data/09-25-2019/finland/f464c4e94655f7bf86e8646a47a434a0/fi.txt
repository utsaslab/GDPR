
 Open public consultation on guidelines on codes of conduct has started


   

19.2.2019
 15.59
| Published in English on 20.2.2019 at 8.56

News item


 European Data Protection Board has compiled the guidelines on codes of conduct. The guidelines have now been published for open consultation. The consultation continues until April 2nd. The Guidelines on Codes of Conduct European Data Protection Board (EDPPB) organise public consultations to gather the views and concerns of all interested stakeholders and citizens. Please note that EDPB might publish your answers on its website. Each consultation will have a privacy statement, which states how your personal data is processed and what rights you have.
