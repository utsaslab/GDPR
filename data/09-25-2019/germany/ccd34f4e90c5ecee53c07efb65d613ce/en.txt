The data protection conference consists of the independent data protection authorities of the federation
and the countries.
Its purpose is to protect and protect the fundamental principles of data protection, to achieve a uniform application of European and national data protection law and to work together for
to continue its development.

The Bavarian State Commissioner for Data Protection checks compliance with data protection regulations at the Bavarian public authorities. He is from the Bavarian
