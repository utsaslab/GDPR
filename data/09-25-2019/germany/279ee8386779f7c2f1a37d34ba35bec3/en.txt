Press release - page 1/2
Munich, 10.05.2019
The Bavarian State Commissioner for Data Protection, Prof. Dr. med. Thomas
Petri, presents his activity report for the years 2017 on May 20, 2019
and 2018 to the public. The report treats as central and
Cross-sectoral data protection law topic the EU data protection reform as well as the resulting adjustments in Germany
and Bavaria.
In addition to overarching topics such as the data protection reform 2018 and the
General Data Protection Regulation, the activity report takes a variety
of topics and individual cases, including:
•

Notifications of data breaches

•

Police task Act

•

Stores in police files

•

Bavarian constitution protection law

•

Transfer forms of the prisons

•

Electronic water meter with radio module

•

Certificate of support for constituency proposals

Your right to privacy - our mission
