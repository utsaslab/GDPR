Invitation to the press conference
Annual Report 2018

On the occasion of the publication of its annual report 2018, the Berlin commissioner for
Data Protection and Freedom of Information, Maja Smoltczyk,
on Thursday, March 28, 2019,
at 11 o'clock
in the press room of the House of Representatives of Berlin (room 190)
to a press conference.
In the Annual Report 2018, the most exciting and important developments in data protection are
summarized from last year:


How has the complaint since the entry into force of the DS-GVO
developed?



What does the DS-GVO on specific legal issues: Is advertising without consent
even allowed? Are restaurants allowed to film their guests? And must
Photographers fines fear if they go about their work?



What has become of the pilot project at Südkreuz station, where "intelligent"
Video technology was tested?



Should I manage my patient data in an app-based health record?



Could parties use data from Deutsche Post for their election campaign?



Are personal data in the databases of Berlin police safe
canceled? And why are they stored there at all?

The Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information is looking forward to working in the
To talk about these and other topics in the press talk and talk to you afterwards
and answer.
