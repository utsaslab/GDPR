01.02.2019Invitation: Safer Internet Day on 5 February 2019 | 01/2019

The Ministry of Justice and for Europe and Consumer Protection and the State Commissioner for Data Protection and the Right to File Access Brandenburg would like to take the Safer Internet Day as an opportunity to act as an actor on the topic "In the Net? to inform. The campaign is also supported by the Verbraucherzentrale Brandenburg.
Time: Tuesday, 5 February 2019, 8:00 am to 10:00 am Venue: In the station of the Potsdam Main Station, Babelsberger Straße 16, 14473 Potsdam
Background: Safer Internet Day 2019 is an annual global action day for more cyber security initiated by the European Union. This year's motto is "Safer Internet Day 2019 - Together for a Better Internet". Here, different players are committed to online safety and a better Internet for children and adolescents. Globally, this day is coordinated by the European Insafe network under the European Commission's CEF Telecom Program. In Germany, klicksafe.de coordinates the day of action and calls for participation in its own actions.
Further information can also be found on the website of the Ministry of Justice and for Europe and Consumer Protection. We would be very happy about your report.
