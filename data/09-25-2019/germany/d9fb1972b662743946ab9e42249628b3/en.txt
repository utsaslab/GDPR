More light! - Making shared responsibility meaningful

The decisions of the European Court of Justice last year
over the joint responsibility were a loud bang, their reverberation
since then has not died. The legal figure of "shared responsibility"
and the related issue, such as such a contractual agreement between
The responsible person involved is actually to be designed, and has since been
Responsible big question marks. The common responsible comes
In this case a key task for the protection of the victims: Decide several
Responsible in common about purposes and means of data processing, so are
they are jointly responsible and must agree with each other, who in the internal relationship fulfills which obligation from the General Data Protection Regulation (DS-GVO).
So far, the design of such a contract, which has been developed by the official regulatory authorities, is being sought in vain
often deprives contractors of important time and personal resources.
With the pattern published by LfDI BW, the first light now comes into the dark. Around
the joint responsibility to make transparent and understandable represents the
LfDI BW now a contract model for shared responsibility under Article 26
DS-GVO available. This pattern was developed on the basis of common considerations with a number of companies and public authorities. Also
At this point LfDI would like to thank Dr. Ing. Stefan Brink for the good cooperation!
