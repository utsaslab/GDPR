In the month of Cyber Security, the Information Commissioner joins outreach activities by issuing guidelines on Internet privacy tools. The guidelines give web users easy instructions on how to protect their personal information stored on different devices, restrict third-party access to their data (and prevent the misuse of their user accounts. The guidelines are useful reading for mobile and social network users who find them useful recommendations such as how to choose and manage good passwords, how to set up two-factor authentication, how to control access to data for advertisers, how to remove hits from search engines, and how to enable privacy-friendly settings in browsers. Mojca Prelesnik, Bachelor of Laws, Information Commissioner
