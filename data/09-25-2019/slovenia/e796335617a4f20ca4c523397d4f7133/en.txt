The Serbian Information Commissioner The Commissioner for Information of Public Importance and Personal Data Protection celebrated the 15th anniversary of the establishment of the Commissioner Institute in Belgrade on June 5, 2019. On this occasion, Information Commissioners Mojca Prelesnik presented a special thank-you plaque for the institutional support and all the assistance IP provided to the Serbian Commissioner, both in the field of personal data protection and in the area of access to public information. The IP plaque means great recognition and commitment to continue successful cooperation in the future.
