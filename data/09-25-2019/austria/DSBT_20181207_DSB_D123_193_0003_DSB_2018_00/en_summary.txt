Section 256 (2) IO further states that access to the insolvency file shall no longer be granted if one year has passed since: - the cancellation of the insolvency proceedings pursuant to §§ 123a, 123b and 139 (Z 1), - the Expiry of the payment period provided for in the recovery plan, if its fulfillment is not monitored (Z 2), - the termination or cessation of monitoring of the recovery plan (Z 3), - the expiration of the payment period provided for in the payment plan (Z 4) or - the early termination or Termination of the levy procedure (point 5).
